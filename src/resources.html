What is conscious menstruation? It refers to the profound awakening that is possible when we embrace our monthly bleeding with deep awareness and self-love.

Find more information at: The power of conscious menstruation

How menstruation works? At this moment, three hundred million women across the planet are experiencing the same thing: a period. The monthly menstrual cycle that gives rise to the period is a reality that most women on Earth will go through in their lives. But why is this cycle so universal? And what makes it a cycle in the first place? Emma Bryce gives a primer on periods.

Find more information at: How menstruation works?

Want to challenge yourself? How much do you know about periods?

What is the purpose of our site? The purpose of our site is to be able to inform women about conscious menstruation. To be able to offer you a careful selection of movies that go according to your mood, being able to inform and entertain.

Lo que si queremos

- Help you understand how you feel.

- Become aware of the menstrual cycle.

- Help to entertain.

- Provide information about conscious menstruation.

- Help you understand how menstruation works.

Lo que no queremos

- We do not want to reinforce stereotypes.

- We reject prejudice.

- We do not want to invalidate you based on your hormones.

- We do not want to generalize.
